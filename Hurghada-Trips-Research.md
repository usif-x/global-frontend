# Hurghada Activities Market Research Report (2026)

## Executive Summary

Hurghada's tourism activity market in 2026 offers exceptional value across all categories, with desert safaris starting from as low as €12 per person and full-day boat trips from €25. The market is characterized by a wide price spectrum between local operators and international booking platforms, with local agencies typically offering 20–40% lower prices for identical experiences.

**Key Market Findings:**

- **Desert Safaris** are the most affordable category, with quad bike tours starting at €18 (shared) and Super Safari combos at €55
- **Boat & Sea Trips** show the widest price variance, from €24 semi-submarine tours to €650+ private yacht charters
- **Water Sports** are highly competitive, with parasailing from €14 and combo packages from $7.63 per person
- **Diving** represents the best value-for-money category, with intro dives from €45 and certified 2-dive days from €60
- **Island Trips** range from $20–$30 for budget options to $80–$100+ for premium experiences
- **Snorkeling** is the most accessible water activity, with shared trips from $20–$35 per adult

**Currency Reference (as of June 2026):**

- 1 USD ≈ 48 EGP (approximate market rate)
- 1 EUR ≈ 52 EGP (approximate market rate)

## Standard Tour Packages

### Desert Safari — Quad Bike Package

**Description:** Experience the thrill of the Eastern Desert on a high-performance ATV. Race across 30–40 km of rolling sand dunes and mountain trails, visit a traditional Bedouin village, and enjoy authentic Bedouin tea. Perfect for adrenaline seekers and culture enthusiasts alike.

- **Duration:** 3–5 hours (morning) / 4–7 hours (sunset with dinner)
- **Pickup:** Hotel pickup and drop-off included (central Hurghada)
- **Equipment included:** Quad bike (single or double seat), safety helmet
- **Food & drinks:** Bedouin tea and mineral water; BBQ dinner with soft drinks (dinner tours only)
- **Common inclusions:** Professional English-speaking guide, Bedouin village stop with tea and bread demonstration, short camel ride, sunset viewpoint (afternoon/evening departures), folkloric entertainment (dinner tours)

**Common Exclusions:** Scarf and goggles rental, optional photo/video packages (€20/USB), tips for guides and camel handlers

---

### Desert Safari — Super Safari Combo

**Description:** The ultimate desert experience combining quad biking, dune buggy driving, Jeep safari, camel riding, and Bedouin culture. This comprehensive 8-hour adventure takes you deep into the Eastern Desert for an unforgettable evening of adrenaline, culture, and cuisine under the stars.

- **Duration:** 7–8 hours
- **Pickup:** Hotel pickup and drop-off included
- **Equipment included:** Quad bike, buggy/spider car, 4×4 Jeep, safety helmets
- **Food & drinks:** Traditional BBQ dinner, soft drinks, Bedouin tea
- **Common inclusions:** Camel ride, sandboarding, Bedouin village visit, folklore show (Tanoura and Belly Dancing), stargazing (evening tours)

**Common Exclusions:** Tips, professional photos/videos, scarf and goggles rental

---

### Boat & Sea — Standard Boat Trip

**Description:** Set sail from Hurghada Marina for a full-day Red Sea adventure. Visit two to three pristine reef sites for snorkeling, enjoy a buffet lunch on board, and soak up the Egyptian sun. Perfect for families, couples, and groups seeking a classic Red Sea experience.

- **Duration:** 7–8 hours
- **Pickup:** Hotel pickup and drop-off included (central Hurghada)
- **Equipment included:** Snorkeling mask, fins, life jacket
- **Food & drinks:** Buffet lunch, soft drinks, tea, coffee
- **Common inclusions:** Two snorkeling stops, beach stop on Giftun Island or Orange Bay, professional guide

**Common Exclusions:** National Park fee ($5/person), island bar drinks (€5–6 for cola), photos/videos, tips, transfer supplements for Makadi Bay/Sahl Hasheesh/El Gouna (€5–15)

---

### Island Trip — Orange Bay / Paradise Island

**Description:** Escape to the turquoise waters of the Giftun Island National Park. Choose between the photogenic sandbars of Orange Bay, the lively entertainment of Paradise Island, or the exclusive tranquility of Mahmya. A perfect blend of beach relaxation and underwater exploration.

- **Duration:** 7–8 hours
- **Pickup:** Hotel pickup and drop-off included
- **Equipment included:** Snorkeling mask, fins, life jacket
- **Food & drinks:** Buffet lunch on boat, soft drinks
- **Common inclusions:** 1–2 snorkeling stops, beach time on island, professional guide

**Common Exclusions:** National Park fee ($5/person mandatory), island bar drinks (€5–6 for cola), photos/videos ($20–30), tips, transfer supplements

---

### Diving — Introductory Dive

**Description:** Discover the underwater wonders of the Red Sea with a professional PADI instructor. No prior experience needed. After a brief safety briefing, you'll make two guided dives at different reef sites, exploring vibrant coral gardens teeming with tropical fish.

- **Duration:** Full day (approx. 7–8 hours)
- **Pickup:** Hotel pickup and drop-off included (central Hurghada)
- **Equipment included:** Full scuba gear (tanks, weights, BCD, regulator, wetsuit, mask, fins) — check if included
- **Food & drinks:** Lunch and soft drinks on board
- **Common inclusions:** PADI-certified instructor, two guided dives, small groups

**Common Exclusions:** Marine/national park fees (€10), dive computer rental (€9), underwater photos/video (€25), tips (€8), transfer supplements for out-of-area hotels

---

### 4. Pricing Strategy Insights

#### How Local Agencies Price vs International Platforms

**Local Egyptian agencies** (Routri, Bella Trips, Blue Corner Red Sea, DiveRED, Infinity Tours):

- **Pricing model:** Direct-to-consumer with minimal commission
- **Average markup:** 0–15% above operational cost
- **Key advantage:** Flexibility, personal service, local knowledge
- **Typical price:** 20–40% lower than international platforms

**International platforms** (Viator, GetYourGuide, Klook):

- **Pricing model:** Commission-based (typically 20–30%)
- **Average markup:** 30–50% above local rates
- **Key advantage:** Brand trust, reviews, easy booking, cancellation policies
- **Typical price:** 30–50% higher than local agencies

**Hotel tour desks:**

- **Pricing model:** Convenience-based with commission
- **Average markup:** 50–100% above local rates
- **Key advantage:** Convenience, charge to room
- **Typical price:** Highest prices in market

#### Where Tourists Overpay

1. **Hotel tour desks** — consistently 50–100% more expensive than local agencies
2. **Last-minute bookings** — limited availability commands premium pricing
3. **Island bar drinks** — €5–6 for a cola on Orange Bay
4. **Professional photos** — €20–30 packages often pushed aggressively
5. **National Park fees** — frequently excluded from advertised prices
6. **Transfer supplements** — outer hotels (Makadi, Sahl Hasheesh, El Gouna) add €5–15
7. **Equipment rental add-ons** — diving gear often priced separately

#### Best-Selling Price Strategy for a Travel Company in Hurghada

**Recommended Pricing Strategy:**

| Tier            | Price Position                    | Strategy                                                                              |
| --------------- | --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Entry Level** | 10–20% above local market minimum | Price transparency; "all-inclusive" messaging; target budget travelers                |
| **Mid-Range**   | Market average                    | Bundle inclusions (photos, park fees, transfers); target value seekers                |
| **Premium**     | 20–30% above average              | VIP service, private groups, flexible itineraries; target quality-conscious travelers |

**Key Recommendations:**

1. **Transparent pricing** — Include National Park fees in base price; this is the #1 traveler complaint
2. **Bundle strategy** — Offer "all-inclusive" packages (fees, photos, tips included) at 15–20% premium
3. **Dynamic pricing** — Peak season (Oct–Apr) can command 10–15% premium
4. **Multi-activity discounts** — Bundle desert + sea activities for 10–20% discount
5. **Private group focus** — Private tours offer 60–108% premium over shared
6. **Digital booking** — Offer online booking with "free cancellation" to compete with international platforms
7. **Local partnerships** — Partner with hotels for referrals without tour desk commission
8. **Add-on strategy** — Offer photos/videos as low-cost add-ons ($10–15) instead of high-margin packages ($20–30)
